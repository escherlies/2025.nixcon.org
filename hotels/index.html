Accommodation

Find the perfect place to stay for NixCon. We've secured special rates at local hotels, highlighted nearby camping options, and provided resources for coordinating shared accommodation with fellow attendees.

Hotels

The Yarn

Breakfast is included.

Travel time by train can be as low as 30 minutes.

Booking: Book via email at corporate@theyarn.ch, mentioning you are attending the NixCon conference.

Hotel Swiss Star

Prices:

  • Single room incl. breakfast CHF 150.00/night
  • Double room incl. breakfast CHF 190.00/night

Travel time by train to OST is about 28 minutes.

Booking: Book via email (info@hotel-swiss-star.ch) or phone (+41 44 578 78 00) and mention promo code "NixCon".

Hotel Residence Loren

Underground parking is available for CHF 10 per night. Cancellation is possible until seven days before arrival.

Prices:

  • Double room for one person incl. breakfast: CHF 125.00/night
  • Double room for two persons incl. breakfast: CHF 150.00/night

Travel time by train to OST can be as low as 37 minutes.

Booking: Book via email at info@hotel-residence-loren.ch and mention "NixCon 2025"

Aabach Hotel

The offer is only valid until the 4th of August. Free parking and a gym are available. A restaurant and a bistro with a quiet sun terrasse are available in the same building.

Prices:

  • Double room for one person incl. breakfast: CHF 145.00/night + CHF 3.5 city tax/night and person
  • Double room for two persons incl. breakfast: CHF 160.00/night + CHF 3.5 city tax/night and person

Travel time by train to OST 35 minutes.

Booking: Book via email at info@aabach-hotel.ch and mention "NixCon 2025"
